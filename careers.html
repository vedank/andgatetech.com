DFT Engineer

Job Locations: Ahmedabad/Bangalore. Experience: 3-8 years

Responsiblity
  • Plan, design, develop, benchmark and deploy E2E test strategies for deep submicron ASIC, SoCs.
  • TEST insertion and ATPG using any of the industry tools - at least SCAN, MBIST, BSCAN; insert/verify/ simulate and deliver RTL/ GLN/ patterns.

  • Hands-on specialist in MBIST, SCAN and BSCAN architecture & insertion to ASIC.
  • Very strong in TEST strategies & fault model fundamentals.
  • Strong exposure to RTL simulation, Physical design work flows, Post-silicon support.
  • Knows to debug test mode timing constraint issues and resolve.
  • Power aware test architecture experience is a huge plus.
  • Specialist in at least one EDA tool, but is tool/methodology/flow agnostic with great functional proven experience.
  • Very good proficiency in at least one programming language like TCL/PERL.
  • Leads by example, works on ambitious, results-oriented schedule & does data driven, flawless aligned decision making.
  • Specialist in collaborating in a team of multi-functional, geographically distributed members with highly diverse skill levels.
  • Requires excellent debug, analytical skills.
  • Bachelors of Engineering/ Master of Technology in E&C/ Electrical/ Computer Science.
